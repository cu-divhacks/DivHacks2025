ABOUT DIVHACKS

DivHacks, founded in 2017, is Columbia University's premier student-led annual diversity hackathon. Over the past seven years, DivHacks has welcomed attendees from all over the tri-state area. We strive to create an empowering and inspirational space for students who are historically underrepresented in the tech industry. Our goal is an experience that not only reimagines what diversity should look like in the tech industry but gives students the tools to use technology to implement change.

TRACKS

Education

Education

From AI-driven tutoring to gamified learning platforms, this is your chance to craft innovative tools that empower students and educators alike. Whether you're passionate about breaking down barriers to education or enhancing classroom engagement, this track is your canvas for creating impactful, tech-driven solutions.

Environment

Environment Track

Address today's most urgent ecological challenges; whether it's leveraging technology for climate change mitigation, or developing tools for biodiversity conservation, this is your chance to make a real impact. This track empowers you to design transformative solutions for a healthier planet.

Health

Health

Tackle pressing health challenges by harnessing technology to enhance patient care, improve mental health, and promote overall well-being. Whether you’re developing apps for chronic disease management, creating solutions for mental health support, or exploring telemedicine advancements, this is your opportunity to help shape a healthier future!

Fashion

Fashion

Address today's most pressing industry challenges! Whether leveraging technology for sustainable fashion, developing tools for circular economies, analyzing ethical production practices, or tracking fast fashion carbon footprints, this track focuses on how to create a more sustainable and ethical fashion industry.

Media

Media

From AI-powered content and personalized media to immersive AR/VR art, this track is for innovators ready to revolutionize news, entertainment, and artistic expression. Tackle challenges like visual analysis, create new storytelling platforms, and reimagine the intersection of art and media in the digital age.

Civic

Civc

Presenting our Civic Track, where tech meets public service! Tackle key civic issues by using innovation to strengthen communities, influence policy, and boost civic involvement. Design platforms for grassroots movements, develop tools for enhancing voter engagement, through this track!
